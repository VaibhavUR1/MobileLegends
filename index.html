Best meta heroes for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang in Season 32

Best Roamer heroes for Season 32

Minotaur

Minotaur is one of the best roamers because he’s tough, can control fights, and can heal himself and his teammates. He can take a lot of damage and stay in fights longer. He can knock up enemies multiple times in a fight, which messes up the enemy team’s plans and helps his own team win. Plus, his healing keeps him and his allies going. This mix of toughness, control, and healing makes him a great pick for roaming.

Mathilda

Mathilda is a top roamer because of her incredible mobility, crowd control, and support abilities. Her skills allow her to move quickly across the map, providing her team with valuable vision and pressure. Her crowd control can disrupt enemies and set up kills for her teammates. Mathilda can also protect her allies and boost their movement speed, making it easier for them to engage in or escape fights. Her ultimate skill lets her dive into battles or provide crucial saves for her team.

Best Exp-Lane heroes for Season 32

Arlott

Arlott is a top EXP laner because of his strong dueling abilities, mobility, and burst damage. His skills have low cooldowns, allowing him to apply constant pressure and keep up his damage output. His crowd control abilities let him lock down opponents, giving him an edge in combat. Arlott’s ultimate skill can make crucial plays by setting up kills for himself or his team, especially with its high burst damage. His mix of damage, mobility, control, and reliable skills makes him a dominant force in the EXP lane.

Best Mid-Lane heroes for Season 32

Vexana

Vexana is one of the best midlaner because she has strong burst damage, crowd control, and a unique ultimate ability. Her skills let her deal a lot of magic damage from a safe distance, making her a major threat in team fights and when poking enemies. She can control the battlefield by disrupting and zoning out opponents with her crowd control abilities. Vexana’s ultimate skill brings back a defeated enemy as a puppet to fight alongside her, which can turn fights around and create more pressure on the opposing team. Her mix of high damage, control, and a game-changing ultimate makes her a top mage pick.

Lylia

Lylia is one of the best midlaners because she’s fast, deals a lot of burst damage, and is great against utility junglers. Her skills let her move quickly around the map and dodge enemy attacks, making her hard to catch. She can hit enemies with big bursts of damage, especially when she combos her abilities well. Lylia’s slow ability can mess up utility junglers’ plans by slowing them down and making it harder for them to do their job. Her ultimate skill, Black Shoes, helps her escape danger and reset her health and cooldowns, giving her more chances to join fights. Her fast wave clearing lets her control the mid lane and help other lanes. Her speed, damage, and ability to counter utility junglers with her slow make her a top choice for the mid lane.

Best Gold Lane heroes for Season 32

Claude

Claude is the best gold laner because he deals a lot of damage, moves around quickly, and clears waves efficiently. His skills let him burst enemies when he gains his items, making him a big threat in fights. His mobility helps him reposition and escape danger, so he can stay safe and keep dealing damage from the backline. Claude’s ultimate, Blazing Duet, deals massive damage to multiple enemies, turning the tide in team fights. His passive ability lets him farm and clear minion waves fast. These strengths make Claude a top choice for the gold lane.

Best Jungle heroes for Season 32

Fredrinn

Fredrinn is the best jungler because of his excellent sustain, crowd control, and versatility. Fredrinn’s crowd control lets him hold enemies in place and set up kills for his team, making him a strong initiator. His ultimate can burst enemies based on how much HP he’s missing, which can surprise and defeat opponents. He’s versatile, so he can dive the enemy backline or protect his allies depending on what’s needed. His mix of damage, control, and sustain makes him a top choice for the jungle role.
